# Swamp · long-form seed-and-compose experiment

Seeded by 20 random words drawn over a 15-line window of Section 1:
chest, wants, Big, twenty, hilt, Nothing, Mimic, shrieks, lunges, arm, check,
move, today, crits, hopeless, jaw, grab, find, lid, knew.

---

The chest sat alone on the only dry hummock for a mile, and every instinct Sila had ever trusted told her to grab it. Bea knew better. Nothing dry survives out here, she said, but Sila was already moving, because the loot wants what the loot wants, and today the loot wanted to be found.

The lid was iron-bound and beautiful and slightly, wrongly warm. Sila crouched, ran a thumb along the seam, and made her check. The seam was not a seam. It was a mouth.

The Mimic lunges. Its lid yawns wide on a hinge of wet teeth, and it catches her by the arm, and its jaw closes with a sound like a door slamming underwater. Sila does not scream. Sila says something Fitz will later insist on leaving out of the ballad. Kwame is already there, big and calm and immovable, planting one boot against the thing's belly and heaving, buying her the half-second she needs.

Hopeless, Bea mutters, meaning all of them, and casts anyway. The firebolt goes wide and hisses into the bog. Fitz's rapier finds a plank. The plank dies a hero's death. For three long breaths the whole soggy world is teeth and shouting and a frantic tug-of-war over one bleeding arm, and it is going badly, and everyone knows it.

Then Sila stops fighting the pull and goes with it instead. She lets the jaw drag her close, finds the soft seam where lid meets throat, and drives her dagger in to the hilt. Natural twenty. The Mimic shrieks, a wet and furious and betrayed sound, and for one bright instant it crits her back out of pure spite, and then it simply comes apart, folding down into a heap of splinters and gold and disappointment. Six coins. One ruined arm. Somewhere in the reeds, the frogs remember how to sing.

Sila wipes her dagger and looks at the wreckage of the chest that wanted so badly to eat her. So, she says. Anyone want to check the next one?
