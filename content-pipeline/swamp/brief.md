# Campaign brief — the Swamp (Dungeon 6)

**Habitat:** a drowned, sinking bog — black water, gas bubbles, cypress knees like
knuckles, everything wet and half-rotted. Sound: frogs that stop all at once.

**Serves text tiers 6–8, boss 9.** The transcript should naturally throw off:
short capitalized sentences (T6), apostrophes/dashes (T7), plain counts and HP/gold
(T8), and dice-notation / exclamations / symbols at the heat-beats (T9).

**Roster → the five sections** (dungeon shape: first branch · waypoint · second branch ·
approach · boss). Each section is one campaign "part," and its harvested lines become that
section's themed prompt pool.

| # | Section        | Beat                                   | Monster(s)              |
|---|----------------|----------------------------------------|-------------------------|
| 1 | First branch   | Into the bog; the too-good-to-be-true chest | **Swamp Mimic** |
| 2 | Waypoint       | A sunken shrine on dry-ish ground; a bargain | **Green Hag** |
| 3 | Second branch  | The rot deepens; the dead don't stay down | **Mummy**, **Wight** |
| 4 | Approach       | Serpent-cult wards guard the black mere | **Yuan-ti Malison** |
| 5 | Boss           | The mere itself rises — many heads      | **Hydra** |

**Throughline:** the party is chasing a rumor of a sunken treasure-vault under the mere.
The Hydra is what's been guarding it. Sila's greed is the engine; Kwame's grit is the
brake; Bea's rules-lawyering is the map; Fitz is the soundtrack; Priya turns the screws.

**Dice:** consumed in order from `../dice.json` at each roll beat — no re-rolls, no mercy.
Inconvenient rolls are kept and written *into* the story (a nat 1 is a disaster the party
has to dig out of, not a do-over).
