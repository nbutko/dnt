# Mountain — full campaign (showrunner output)

Cast: MAB (GM), BRONT (Fighter), QUILL (Wizard), SABLE (Rogue), FIFE (Bard).
Dice ledger — d20 consumed in order: 3, 20, 4, 20, 17, 16, 8, 19, 15, 20, 8, 9 (§1); 4, 14, 6, 11, 8, 12, 19, 11, 1, 13, 15, 9 (§2); 6, 8, 9, 8, 13, 15, 10, 14, 3, 12, 4 (§3); 6, 8, 14, 4, 7, 10, 19, 10, 7, 2, 16 (§4); 20, 15, 20, 17, 17, 5, 12, 10, 17, 6, 14, 15, 8, 12, 12, 3, 12, 7, 19, 2, 2, 12, 19, 3, 8, 12 (§5). d8/d6/d4 consumed in order for damage.

Throughline: The party has climbed three days for a rumor — that above the clouds, at the Monastery of the Ninth Wind, hangs the **Peaksong Bell**: ring it once and every avalanche on the mountain freezes mid-fall, every locked door in the range clicks open, and (the part Sable cares about) the monks' vault unseals. The mountain does not want the bell rung. Neither does the thing coiled around the bell-tower.
