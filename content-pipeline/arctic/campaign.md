# The Arctic — full campaign (showrunner output)

Cast: MAB (GM), BRONT (Fighter), QUILL (Wizard), SABLE (Rogue), FIFE (Bard).
Dice ledger: d20 consumed in order — 5, 16, 14, 3, 17, 9, 13, 3, 16, 19 (§1); 10, 12, 1, 11, 9, 2, 11 (§2); 19, 19, 14, 17, 1, 14 (§3); 20, 20, 5, 8, 3, 14, 9, 7, 20 (§4); 11, 7, 9, 18, 3, 13, 13, 1, 4, 18, 19, 10, 20, 1, 7, 3, 1, 13 (§5). d8/d6/d4 spent inline for damage.

Throughline: In a dockside tavern the party bought a rumor for 40 gold — that the whaling ship *Gull's Mercy*, lost eleven winters ago, froze solid around a relic called the Ember of Deep-Winter, a stone that never goes cold. Whoever holds it never freezes, never starves, never fears the dark. The wreck lies four days out across the pack ice. What the drunk who sold them the rumor forgot to mention: something enormous, white, and patient has been sleeping on top of it.

## §2 Waypoint — "The Gull's Mercy" (Young Remorhaz)

Frozen ropes hang in mid-swing. A frozen gull, wings spread, is locked into the ice of the mainmast where it struck eleven winters ago. ## §4 Approach — "The Purring Dark" (Abominable Yeti)
