## §3 Second Branch — "Heat Where There Should Be None" (Fire Giant)

## §4 Approach — "The Water That Thinks" (Aboleth)

The Chime, cupped in Sable's hands, throws its clean light ahead of you now, and where its daylight touches the walls the fungus recoils and curls — this glow was made to be up *there*, in the sun, and everything down here hates it. The stair ends at the lip of an underground lake, black and utterly still, so still it is a perfect mirror, so that you seem to stand at the top of a second party descending into a second cavern below.

**MAB:** And the water is warm. That's wrong — everything else is cold, but the lake steams faintly, and it *smells*, a low green rot-and-fish reek that coats the back of your throat. ## §5 Boss — "The One Who Was Waiting for the Light" (Mind Flayer Arcanist)

**MAB:** The passage climbs out of the wet and delivers you into a chamber that does not belong in a cave. The floor is polished to black glass. The walls are lined with jars — hundreds of them, floor to unseen ceiling, and in each jar floats a single brain, pale and slick and softly pulsing, and each one is wired by silver thread to the next, so the whole room hums with a low, wet, thinking sound. In the center of the room, on a throne grown from the same black glass, something rises to greet you, and it has been expecting you since before you left the surface.

**MAB:** It is a **Mind Flayer Arcanist** — a mauve, robed figure, human in shape and nothing else, with a head like a hooded octopus and four long tentacles framing a lamprey mouth.
