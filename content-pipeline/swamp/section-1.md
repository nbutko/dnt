# Swamp · Section 1 (First Branch) — "The Rude Box"

Encounter: **Swamp Mimic**. Dice consumed in order from `../dice.json`:
d20 → 1, 13, 5, 9, 20, 20 · d8 → 1 · d6 → 1, 1.

---

**PRIYA (GM):** You smell it before you see it. The trail ends in black water. Cypress knees jut from the muck like knuckles.
**PRIYA:** Somewhere ahead, every frog stops singing at once.
**KWAME:** I don't like quiet.
**SILA:** I love quiet. Quiet is where the money is.
**PRIYA:** A boardwalk sags across the bog. Half the planks are gone. On a dry hummock in the middle sits a chest — iron-bound, waiting.
**SILA:** Oh, hello, gorgeous.
**BEA:** Sila, wait. Nothing dry survives out here — that's statistically impossible.
**FITZ:** And yet the heart wants what it wants! Onward, to riches!
**PRIYA:** Give me a check to pop the lock, Sila. `[d20 → 1]`
**SILA:** ...it's a one, isn't it.
**PRIYA:** The lid yawns open on its own. Rows of wet teeth. The chest lunges and clamps down on your arm. Swamp Mimic — roll for initiative.
**BEA:** I knew it! Mimic: 39 HP, resistant to acid, and its grab is a DC 13 to escape!
**KWAME:** Big box. Small problem. `[d20 → 13, hit; d8 → 1]` I hit it. Barely.
**FITZ:** Fear not, friends — Fitz the Undaunted strikes! `[d20 → 5, miss]` My rapier finds a plank. The plank is defeated.
**BEA:** You are hopeless. Firebolt — `[d20 → 9, miss]` and it hisses wide into the water.
**SILA:** Fine. Nobody move. `[d20 → 20]`
**PRIYA:** Natural twenty. You find the seam where the lid meets the jaw and drive the dagger in to the hilt.
**SILA:** That's for my arm. `[crit! d6 → 1, d6 → 1]`
**BEA:** Even a low crit is 2d6 doubled + your DEX — that's 8 damage!
**PRIYA:** The Mimic shrieks, a wet furious sound, and bites down harder. `[d20 → 20]` Natural twenty — it crits you back, Sila.
**SILA:** Of course it does. Why would today be easy?
**KWAME:** Hold on. I've got you.
**PRIYA:** Kwame plants a boot on its lid and heaves. The teeth come loose with your arm still attached. You're 6 gold richer, and the frogs have not started singing again.
**FITZ:** A glorious victory! I shall call it the Ballad of the Rude Box!
**SILA:** Call it whatever. How much do we think the teeth are worth?
